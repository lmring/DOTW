Resources

Official Character Sheets

Printable character sheets in the official 5E format. You will need to fill one of these out to play D&D--just transfer the information from your Story Sheet!

Click here to get an official printable character sheet!

“Basic” Rules

Digital character sheet creation. Don’t want to print out an official character sheet? Make an account on Mythweavers and fill everything out online! You can even link your character sheet to a fellow player or to your DM!

Click here for online character sheet creation!

Turn Guides

Reminders of what you can do on each turn of combat. Click each icon for more details!

Click here for a guide on what to do when it's your turn in combat!

Virtual Dice Roller

This website allows you to roll as many dice as you want! Very useful for if you don’t have physical dice, can’t find them, or just want something more convenient.

Click here for an online dice roller!

Point Buy Calculator

A calculator for the “point buy” method of character attribute selection. Use this once you have chosen your race to determine your final statistics and modifiers!

Click here to use the Point Buy Calculator!

“Basic” Rules

Official “basic” rules provided by Wizards of the Coast, the creators of D&D. This is good reference material, but it’s 114 pages long!

Click here for the official "basic" rules!

Roll20

A platform for playing D&D digitally! Everything is included in the Roll20 system, from dice rolling to map creation. You can even search information about spells and items! This is a great resource for people who need a run a campaign but not everyone can make it. Be sure to show your DM this great resource!

Click here to play tabletop games online!
