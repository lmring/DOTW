Resources

Official Character Sheets

Printable character sheets in the official 5E format. You will need to fill one of these out to play D&D--just transfer the information from your Story Sheet!

http://dnd.wizards.com/articles/features/character_sheets

Virtual Dice Roller

This website allows you to roll as many dice as you want! Very useful for if you don’t have physical dice, can’t find them, or just want something more convenient.

https://www.wizards.com/dnd/dice/dice.htm

Point Buy Calculator

A calculator for the “point buy” method of character attribute selection. Use this once you have chosen your race to determine your final statistics and modifiers!

http://chicken-dinner.com/5e/5e-point-buy.html

“Basic” Rules

Official “basic” rules provided by Wizards of the Coast, the creators of D&D. This is good reference material, but it’s 114 pages long!

https://media.wizards.com/2015/downloads/dnd/BasicRules_Playerv3.4.pdf

Turn Guides

Reminders of what you can do on each turn of combat. Click each icon for more details!

https://crobi.github.io/dnd5e-quickref/preview/quickref.html

Roll20

A platform for playing D&D digitally! Everything is included in the Roll20 system, from dice rolling to map creation. You can even search information about spells and items!

https://roll20.net/

Mythweavers

Digital character sheet creation. Don’t want to print out an official character sheet? Make an account on Mythweavers and fill everything out online!

https://www.myth-weavers.com/
